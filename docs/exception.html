The Carwash Caper

I made an exception.

I had no idea what was so "urgent" that she couldn't pay up, but I figured she was probably using her car as collateral without actually telling me it was collateral.

"Uhm... I guess I can make an exception for you... if you're really that desperate," I said.
"Oh, thank you, sweetheart!" she doted. "I'll be back as soon as I can, 'kay?"

She trotted away up the lane and out of sight, leaving me to do whatever I wanted with her ever so lovely vehicle. I was tempted to take a page out of my favorite movie's playbook and take the car for a joyride, but knowing that I couldn't easily roll back the odometer, I swiftly tucked the idea away.

I grabbed the soap nozzle from off of the wall and was ready to pull the trigger before I realized that the lady left the car running. Something like that isn't usually a big deal since people will stay in their car while I work, but it just wasn't quite sitting right with me. I didn't want to cause too much more trouble for when she came back, but it's not like she couldn't turn it back on, either. I gave it some thought...
