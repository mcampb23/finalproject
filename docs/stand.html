A Space Ranger's Rodeo

You decided to stand your ground!

You aren't going to let a few pesky robot aliens get in the way of your mission, so you dive into cover and ready yourself for combat. Blaster fire is soaring above your head, but you crawl into another position while they're still distracted by where they think you are. You pop out of cover and fire your blaster, and you manage to take out half of the robots before those that remain lock back onto your position.

They're not a very mobile bunch, preferring to stay in one spot and wait for you, but you don't have time to wait for them. You duck back into and cover to triangulate their exact position in the room. You need to take the rest of them out in one fell swoop, and there's no better way to do that than with the stalactite that just so happens to be hanging above their sorry metal heads. You aim your blaster and place three well timed shots around the circumference of the stalactite, watching as it starts to crack and tear itself free from the ceiling.

In less than a moment, it plunges down from above and crashes down on top of the remaining robots. The sounds of blaster fire come to an end, and you stand back up to your feet to secure the room. With no more threats headed your way, you breathe a sigh of relief. You figure it'd be a good idea to start collecting some of the materials in the room, but there's also the matter of where the robot aliens even came from in the first place. You weigh the options and decide...
