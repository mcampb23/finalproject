The Carwash Caper

I braved the crowd.

As much as I didn't want to make a scene, it was probably better that I did instead of letting that lady do whatever she wanted. I dove into the crowd and began searching for any pretty brunette wearing red that I could find. There was a lot of bumping into people and pushing them off of me, but after going around in what felt like circles just to find her, I caught a glimpse of her standing behind who I could only assume was the groom. She was pickpocketing a thumbdrive from his coat pocket, and I guess he was too enamored with his newlywed wife to notice.

She dropped it inside of her handbag and casually stepped away, mingling back in with the rest of the crowd. I pushed my way through everyone that was in my way until I finally caught up with her. I latched my hand around her bag and pulled it back, causing her to almost lose her balance. The lady glanced behind her and looked around until she dropped her gaze to me.

"Watcha got in there?" I asked her over the noise.
